Peg City Dharma Bums

"Your work is to discover your world and then with all your heart give yourself to it."


-Buddha

Peg City Dharma Bums is a Winnipeg-based sangha that digs meditation and pluralistic thinking. While unsolicited donations are always welcome, our study sessions are open to the public and available at no cost.

Instructors

Dianne Dharma has spent three decades across four continents studying with renowned teachers from some of the world's most celebrated wisdom traditions.

Molly Mindfulness hasn't been in the game for as long as Dianne, but she has been on retreat once or twice, and has good availability in the evenings.

Meetings

We meet every week at 19 Home Street between 7 p.m. and 9 p.m. on Wednesdays and 12 p.m. and 2 p.m. on Saturdays. Walk-ins are welcome, or feel free to touch base first through our contact section.
